What is the Poker Companion App?

The Poker Companion App is a project I created with the intention of tracking and collecting data about players you play online poker with and analyzing that data to provide insights about your opponents tendancies and what their actions might mean. The goal is to process the data collected to give the user a better idea of if their opponents are bluffing or if they might fold to a bluff.
